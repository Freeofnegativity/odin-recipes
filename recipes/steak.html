Blakey's Big Boy Steak

How not to ruin a steak...

Tired of well done pieces of leather? Read on...

Ingredients

  • 1x thickish cut steak - no more than 2.5cm/1" thick.
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 knob of butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Fresh rosemary

Recipe

  1. Bring steak to room temperature by removing from fridge at least 30 minutes prior to cooking.
  2. Pat dry and season the steak generously with salt and pepper to help form a great crust when searing the steak.
  3. Heat a frying pan to a high temperature.
  4. Add knob of butter and garlic cloves to pan, allowing the butter to coat the entirety of the surface of the pan, then add the steak.
  5. Flip steak every minute until cooked. I find to reach medium it takes around 3 minutes 30 seconds.

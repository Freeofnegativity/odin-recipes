Blakey's Bodacious Burgers

How to craft a good burger...

Within this recipe you will find all of the burger goodness required to recreate your very own Five Guys-inspired burger.

Ingredients

  • 1x eye-waterin' onion
  • 1x bank-destroyin' brioche bun
  • 1x juicy steak burger (no Birdseye pucks)
  • 3+ many slices of cheese (or as many as you can fit)
  • 2 pickle slices (to show willing)
  • A good dollop of condiments (ketchup and mustard is the GOAT)

Recipe

  1. Chop up onion and pickles.
  2. Add oil and onions to a moderate heat frying pan, and fry off onions until golden.
  3. Once onions are fried, remove from pan, and place brioche buns face down in pan to toast.
  4. Once bun is fried, remove, and reapply oil to pan before adding burger.
  5. Fry burger for 5 minutes, ensuring to turn regularly in order to cook evenly.
  6. Once burgers are cooked, remove from pan, and apply condiments and pickles to base of burger bun.
  7. Lay steak burger on top of condiments.
  8. Lay slice of cheese on top of the steak burger, and then add more condiments on top.
  9. Finally, add top of burger bun on top.
  10. Pro Tip: to really get your burgers moist and juicy like Five Guys, wrap finished burger in foil, and heat in air fryer for 5 minutes on a low heat.
